# Decision Log

A running log of architectural decisions, their rationale, and open items.

This file is owned by the Architect role. Entries must be added when a significant architectural, data model, backend interface, or product decision is made — not after implementation.

## How to use this log

- Add a new row to the Decisions table when a decision is confirmed.
- Add a new row to Open Items when a question is deferred or unresolved.
- Prefix open item IDs with `OI-` to distinguish them from decision IDs.
- Date format: DD Mon YYYY (e.g. 12 May 2026).
